Info

Arithmetic Operations:

 • Addition (+): Add two numbers.

 • Subtraction (-): Subtract two numbers.

 • Multiplication (×): Multiply two numbers.

 • Division (÷): Divide one number by another.


Advanced Functions:

 • Square Root (√): Calculate the square root of a number by pressing the √ button.

 • Exponentiation (y^x): Raise a number to the power of another number using the ^ button.

 • Reciprocal (1/x): Calculate the reciprocal of a number.

 • Parentheses (( and )): Use parentheses to group operations and control the order of execution.


Constants:

 • Pi (π): Insert the constant π (approximately 3.14159).

 • Euler's Number (e): Insert the constant e (approximately 2.71828).
